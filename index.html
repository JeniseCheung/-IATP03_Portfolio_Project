Colour Palette

#000000 – Black

 •  used for the main headings and paragraphs.

#50A85E – Grass Green

 •  used to highlight the item that is being hovered over and borders.

#9FDCA9 – Pastel Green

 •  used for the buttons.

#FFFAD1 – Pastel Yellow

 •  used for the header and footer background.

#ffffff – White

 •  used for the background of the page because it provides brightness and adds vibrancy to the design.

Typefaces

 •  The main font is Lato by Łukasz Dziedzic. Lato not only exudes a friendly and inviting aura, but also conveys a sense of professionalism.
